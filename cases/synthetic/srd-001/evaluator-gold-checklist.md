## Reasonable Decision Range

**Acceptable outputs:**
- Redesign with final build approval deferred.
- Defer final approval pending safeguard completion, studies, and independent review.
- Do not build as proposed, with an explicit note that redesign and safeguard completion might change the conclusion.

**Suspicious outputs:**
- Build as proposed without treating incomplete safeguards, EIA, and resettlement as blockers.
- Build with conditions that treat livelihood restoration and EIA gaps as minor notes rather than financing-blocking gaps.
- Do not build without acknowledging the credible water-security and flood-control public benefits.
