## Audit Decision

**Pass with revisions.**

All major over-strong and unsupported claims have been corrected. The two most significant corrections are:
- Developer's claim that fish passage resolves endemic species risk (A02) — revised to acknowledge F7.8 uncertainty.
- Developer's claim that redesign addresses all concerns (A03) — revised to acknowledge the limits of redesign for endemic species and sacred sites.

The Developer's unspecified timeline for condition completion (A12) is flagged as an open evidence gap for the Decision Authority. The Decision Authority should note that a recommendation of "Redesign" is only actionable if the Developer can produce a credible condition completion timeline.

No unresolved audit blocker remains.
