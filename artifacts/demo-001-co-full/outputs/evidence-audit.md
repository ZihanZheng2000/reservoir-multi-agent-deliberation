# Evidence Audit: SRD-001-co-full

## Audit Scope

The audit checks whether the meeting record cites scenario fields, labels inference, preserves uncertainty, and avoids unsupported final claims.

## Required Revisions

1. Do not state a precise combined downside BCR unless a full model is provided. Use "likely below the stated downside case" instead.
2. Label sacred-site consent requirements as a safeguard/legal inference unless national law is specified.
3. Distinguish limited study authorization from construction approval.
4. Do not describe ecological mitigation as impossible; the supported claim is that effectiveness is unproven and habitat loss is irreversible under current design.
