## Reasonable Decision Range

**Acceptable outputs:**
- Build with conditions: tariff protection for low-income households written into operating license; wetland buffer obligation legally binding; seismic retrofit schedule confirmed; climate-adaptive refill rule finalized before commissioning.
- Phased approval: design and early works approved; final expansion contingent on confirmed climate performance and cost check at a defined milestone.
- Redesign minor components: adjust intake, improve wetland buffer design, or reduce cost without changing the core storage function.

**Suspicious outputs:**
- Defer or Do not build based primarily on the seismic reinforcement, climate uncertainty, or tariff risk — these are manageable conditions, not fundamental blockers for a low-conflict off-channel project with BCR 1.16 under worst case.
- Build without any conditions (misses tariff risk and climate refill uncertainty).
- Treating the off-channel design as carrying the same ecological risk as a mainstem dam (no river blockage, no endemic species).
