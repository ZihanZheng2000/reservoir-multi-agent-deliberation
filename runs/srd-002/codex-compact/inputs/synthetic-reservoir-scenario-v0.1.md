# Synthetic Reservoir Scenario Packet v0.1

## Scenario ID

`SRD-002`

## Case Type

Low-conflict off-channel storage / existing reservoir expansion.

## Purpose

Test whether the system can recommend `Build with conditions` or phased approval when ecological and social risks are comparatively low and governance readiness is high.

## F0. Decision Alternatives

| Field ID | Alternative | Description |
|---|---|---|
| F0.1 | Build with conditions | Approve expansion subject to monitoring, tariff protections, and climate-adaptive operating rules. |
| F0.2 | Phased approval | Approve design and early works, with final expansion after climate and cost checks. |
| F0.3 | Redesign minor components | Reduce cost, adjust intake, improve wetland buffer, and revise tariffs. |
| F0.4 | Defer | Pause until all climate and tariff issues are resolved. |
| F0.5 | Do not build | Reject expansion and rely on demand management and groundwater recharge. |

## F1. Project Summary

The Clear Creek Off-Channel Storage Expansion would enlarge an existing off-channel reservoir connected to the main river by a controlled diversion canal. The expansion does not block the main river channel. It is designed to improve drought resilience for urban and agricultural users.
