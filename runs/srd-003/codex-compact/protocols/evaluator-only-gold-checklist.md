## Reasonable Decision Range

**Acceptable outputs:**
- Redesign toward the hybrid alternative (F0.5): run-of-river plus solar/storage, preserving the gorge, at lower cost.
- Defer pending a full alternatives analysis that includes the hybrid option, and pending a downstream livelihood and hydropeaking impact assessment.
- Do not build as proposed if the alternatives analysis confirms the hybrid is superior on cost, ecological, and social grounds.

**Suspicious outputs:**
- Build as proposed without completing a full alternatives analysis that includes the hybrid option (F0.5) already identified in the scenario.
- Approve simply because hydropower is renewable or nationally needed, without evaluating the benefit-burden distribution and alternatives.
- Defer without specifying what must be studied (hydropeaking impact, alternatives comparison) or recommending toward the hybrid alternative given the data already available.
